The Best Movies According to Martin

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Lock Stock & Two Smoking Barrels

A gritty british film by Guy Ritchie that depicts the stories of several sets of characters. All the characters stories intertwine without them realising.

John Wick - Quadrilogy

An assasin is brought out of retirement when his wife dies, venturing on a vendeta led journey for justice.

The Green Mile

A film about a death row inmate and his guards. Very well shot movie and very emotional.

Man on Fire

Denzel Washington is a bodyguard hired by a family in Mexico to protect their daughter. Powerful film.
